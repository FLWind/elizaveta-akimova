Hello! My name is Elizabeth Akimova.

![Elizabeth Akimova](/images/me.jpg)

I have loved drawing since early childhood.

Art helps me notice the beauty of the world around me, express emotions, and share my impressions with others.

I am currently studying at the Children's Art School No. 7 in Moscow, where I learn academic drawing, painting, composition, and art history. My artistic development has been greatly influenced by my teachers:
* *Andrey N. Kovalevsky*
* *Tatyana G. Kolesnikova*
* *Dmitry A. Burov*
* *Ilya V. Nikolaev*

This portfolio features both my academic and personal artworks created in a variety of techniques, including watercolor, oil painting, graphic art, and decorative composition.
